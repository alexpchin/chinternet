Tips to learn coding?!

  1. Everyone can code. Everyone.
  2. Computers are dumb but they don't make mistakes.
  3. Don't try to learn everything at once. You can make a cake without knowing how the oven works.
  4. To become good at coding, you need to enjoy it. Have fun!
  5. Teach other people as much as possible.
